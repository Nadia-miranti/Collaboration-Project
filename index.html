ISSUE : THE IMPORTANCE OF HAVING BREAKFAST

Breakfast is often called the most important meal of the day, yet many people still skip it. Having breakfast plays a vital role in maintaining our health and improving our daily performance. It provides the body with the necessary nutrients and energy to start the day properly. Therefore, having breakfast should be considered a daily habit for everyone, especially students and workers.
Firstly, breakfast gives our body the energy it needs after a long night of fasting. When we sleep, our body continues to use energy for basic functions such as breathing and maintaining body temperature. By the time we wake up, our energy level is low. Eating breakfast helps refill our energy storage and prepares us to face activities throughout the day without feeling weak or tired.

Secondly, breakfast improves concentration and academic performance. Studies show that students who eat breakfast are more focused, active, and perform better in school than those who skip it. When the brain receives enough glucose from food, it can function more effectively, improving memory and attention span. Without breakfast, it becomes harder to concentrate and stay alert during morning lessons or work meetings.


Furthermore, having breakfast helps control body weight. People who skip breakfast often feel hungrier later in the day, leading them to eat larger portions or choose unhealthy snacks. In contrast, a balanced breakfast with protein, fiber, and vitamins helps reduce cravings and keeps appetite stable. In addition, eating breakfast can improve metabolism, allowing the body to burn calories more efficiently throughout the day. This means that having a proper breakfast not only prevents overeating but also supports a healthy and active lifestyle.


In conclusion, breakfast is essential for maintaining energy, enhancing brain performance, and managing weight. Skipping breakfast may seem like saving time, but it can negatively affect both health and productivity. A simple breakfast such as fruit, eggs, or whole-grain bread can already provide great benefits for our body. Therefore, we should develop the habit of eating breakfast every morning to stay strong, focused, and ready to achieve our daily goals.

POSITIVE IMPACT

  • Increases energy and prevents fatigue.

  • Improves focus and brain performance.

  • Helps control body weight and metabolism.

NEGATIVE IMPACT

  • Skipping breakfast causes tiredness and lack of concentration.

  • Unhealthy breakfast can lead to weight gain or sleepiness.

  • Irregular breakfast habits may cause stomach discomfort or poor digestion.
